Combined and Expanded Exercise Solutions

by

Saar Barak

LaTeX documents available on GitHub


Basic combinatorics is an important topic in mathematics that deals with counting and arranging objects. This website provides a collection of exercises and solutions for anyone who wants to learn and practice combinatorics.

The exercises are organized by topics such as permutations, combinations, and binomial coefficients. Each exercise includes a detailed solution with step-by-step explanations and examples. The solutions are written in LaTeX and can be downloaded from GitHub.

Whether you are a student, a teacher, or a math enthusiast, this website can help you improve your combinatorial skills and deepen your understanding of this fascinating field of mathematics. Happy counting!
